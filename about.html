Nice to meet you!

My Name is Ignacio, and I come from Sevilla, Spain.

I am a JavaScript developer with a background in sales and account management.

How I got here

Having studied Tourism at University of Sevilla and taking an MBA at ThepowerMBA, I made my way to the IT sector working for trivago N.V., where I became account manager for hotel chains and travel agencies for the Spanish and ROW market. Once inside a TECH company, I understood the potential of the industry, and that is the reason why I made my move into the tech world by becoming a web developer, and studying during my free time Computer Science at Brilliant.org.

What I offer

What I contribute to a development team, in addition to my technical skills, is my background in sales: having worked as an account manager for software services, I collaborated with developers to identify solutions and ensure deadlines with the products were met; I negotiated long term contracts with high value customers and dealt with hundreds of their challenges; and last but not least, l learned to provide feedback to the product team, the different methods and how to make it effectively.

As a result, I am able to collaborate across teams, recognize patterns, provide quick fixes and come up with long-term strategies.

To sum up, I am a motivated, fresh developer, with a different background and transferable skills that set a different view in the team and still fulfills all the technical requirements needed.
